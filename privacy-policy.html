Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 3, 2026

AVOMA ("we," "us," or "our") provides AI receptionist services to local service businesses. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information gathered through our services when you interact with a business powered by AVOMA.

1. Who We Are

AVOMA is an AI automation service that operates AI receptionists on behalf of local service businesses. When you call a business powered by AVOMA, your interaction is handled by our system. We are headquartered at 15 Harvey Lane, Westborough, MA 01581.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you interact with our AI receptionist:

  • Your name and phone number (from your inbound call)
  • The nature of your inquiry or appointment request
  • Call duration and outcome
  • Appointment details (date, time, service requested)
  • SMS message history related to your inquiry

We do not collect payment information, Social Security numbers, or sensitive personal identifiers.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Confirm and remind you of appointments you have scheduled
  • Send follow-up messages related to your inquiry or missed call
  • Maintain call and appointment records for the business you contacted
  • Improve the accuracy and performance of our AI receptionist

4. SMS Messaging

By calling a business powered by AVOMA, you consent to receive SMS messages related to your inquiry or appointment. These messages may include appointment confirmations, reminders, missed call follow-ups, and re-engagement messages.

Message and data rates may apply. Message frequency varies based on your appointment activity — typically 1–3 messages per interaction. Reply STOP to opt out at any time. Reply HELP for assistance.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your information is stored securely in our CRM system hosted on PostgreSQL/Supabase infrastructure with encrypted connections. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is shared only with:

  • The specific business you contacted (to fulfill your appointment or inquiry)
  • Infrastructure providers necessary to operate our service (e.g., Twilio for SMS, Vapi for calls)

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying STOP

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@callavoma.com.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of services powered by AVOMA constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
