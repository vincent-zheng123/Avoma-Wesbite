1. SMS Program Description

AVOMA operates an SMS messaging program on behalf of local service businesses. Messages sent through this program include:

  • Appointment confirmations

  • No-show re-engagement messages
  • General follow-up messages related to your inquiry

2. How to Opt In

You opt in to receive SMS messages by calling a business powered by AVOMA and verbally consenting to receive a follow-up text message during the call. Consent is recorded in our system at the time of the call.

3. How to Opt Out

Reply STOP to any message to opt out immediately. You will receive one final confirmation message and no further messages will be sent to your number.

For help, reply HELP or contact us at support@callavoma.com. Opting out will not affect your appointment — please contact the business directly if you need to cancel or reschedule.
